Paranormal

The elusive and mysterious, a ghost is a spectral presence that exists beyond the realm of the living. Often associated with the ethereal plane, these apparitions are said to be the remnants of departed souls, lingering in the world with unfinished business or unfulfilled destinies. Ghost stories have woven a tapestry of intrigue and fear across cultures, with tales of haunted places and spectral encounters captivating the human imagination. Whether portrayed as benevolent guardians, tormented spirits, or mischievous entities, the concept of ghosts continues to evoke a sense of wonder, curiosity, and a touch of the supernatural in our collective consciousness.
